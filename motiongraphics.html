Motion Graphics

Prerequisite: MTM6200

Course Description

Motion graphics, typography, special effects and animation are used in the industry to market and sell products and services. Students enhance existing video footage and create brand or product-based animation sequences through industry-standard motion graphics software packages. Students work with multiple forms of media, syncing together audio, video, transitions and added effects to create professional quality video.

Course Topics

  • File Organisation
  • Basic Animation Techniques
  • Rendering & Exporting
  • Pre-composition
  • Shapes & Masking
  • Introduction to 3D animation
  • Green Screen Techniques
  • Motion Tracking & Video Stabilisation
  • Puppeting
